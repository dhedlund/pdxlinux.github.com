    News and discussion forum focusing on Linux and related issues. One of the best news sites for what is cuting edge in the world of Linux and Open Source software.

    Another newsite. Older articles are a rich resource on the happenings in the Linux world.

    "Biting the hand that feeds IT." Also available in a US version. While not solely newsite covering Linux, worth reading if for no other reason than the biting British wit used in describing and commenting on technology developments. Also home for the latest BOFH stories.

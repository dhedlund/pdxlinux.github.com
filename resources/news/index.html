• Advogato
    Discussion forums and journals of Open Source developers.
  • Desktoplinux
    News of Linux for the desktop.
  • Freshmeat
    The most complete website for free *nix software. New releases and updates for software are constantly posted.
  • Kuro5hin
    A technology discussion forum like Slashdot, but in recent years it has focussed more on political topics than technological.
  • Linux and Main
    A new entry into the Linux newsites arena.
  • Linux Gazette
    "Making Linux just a little more fun." Lots of howto articles.
  • Linux Today
    News and discussion forum focusing on Linux and related issues. One of the best news sites for what is cuting edge in the world of Linux and Open Source software.
  • Linux Weekly News
    Weekly survey of what's happening in the Linux world, with daily updates.
  • LinuxPlanet
    Another newsite. Older articles are a rich resource on the happenings in the Linux world.
  • NewsForge: Open Source News
  • Slashdot
    Their motto: News for Nerds. Stuff that matters.
  • The Register
    "Biting the hand that feeds IT." Also available in a US version. While not solely newsite covering Linux, worth reading if for no other reason than the biting British wit used in describing and commenting on technology developments. Also home for the latest BOFH stories.
